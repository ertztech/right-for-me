# Career Brain

## Purpose

Career Brain is the durable source of truth for reusable career data that can support resumes, application packets, interview prep, and future outputs.

## What Career Brain Owns

Career Brain owns durable career data and the reusable structure behind the user's professional story.

Early conceptual data areas:

- experiences
- roles
- skills
- achievements
- stories
- evidence
- timeline
- goals
- preferences
- values
- metadata

## What Career Journey Owns

Career Journey owns the guided experience layer that helps a user uncover, shape, and organize information. It is responsible for the chapter-based flow, progress framing, and first-time or returning-user experience patterns.

## Principle

Career Journey can collect and shape information, but Career Brain owns durable career data.

## Non-Goals For Now

- no full schema
- no persistence implementation
- no AI extraction design yet
