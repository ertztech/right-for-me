# North Star

## Mission

Help people confidently pursue better careers by understanding each opportunity and generating everything needed to apply well.

## Product Direction

NextMove is an AI-powered career operating system, not just a resume builder. The resume is one output of a broader workflow that helps someone decide where to apply, position themselves honestly, and prepare for interviews.

## User Outcome

Someone should be more likely to choose the right opportunity and get an interview because NextMove helps them:

- capture their career story once
- understand role fit quickly
- uncover evidence they can use
- generate honest, reusable application outputs
- keep momentum from opportunity review through follow-up

## Product Center Of Gravity

- The user's career profile, story bank, or Career Brain is the reusable source of truth.
- Opportunities become workspaces where job-specific analysis and outputs come together.
- AI should reduce repetitive work and uncover useful stories, not force blank-form data entry.
