# Parking Lot Review Workflow

Purpose:
Keep the parking lot intentionally small and useful.

Reference example:
- ../examples/parking-lot-review-example.md

Referenced roles:
- ../agents/facilitator-orchestrator.md
- ../agents/product-owner.md
- ../product-direction.md

Decisions:
- Promote
- Keep
- Archive
- Discard

Rules:
- The parking lot is for deferred ideas, not permanent clutter.
- Promote only items that now have a clearer reason to exist.
- Archive items that may matter later but do not deserve current attention.
- Discard items that no longer support product direction.
- Keep the parking lot intentionally small.

Required Output:
1. Promote
2. Keep
3. Archive
4. Discard
5. Agreements
6. Disagreements
7. Confidence
8. Decision-required items
